Sema4 is a next-generation health information company, spun out of the Mount Sinai Health System, that provides advanced genomic testing and merges big data analytics with clinical diagnostics. Our team creates practical tools that help patients, clinicians, and researchers better diagnose, treat, and prevent disease. We are striving to construct a more comprehensive picture of health by integrating three key elements: a wealth of clinical experience that informs the answers that patients and providers are seeking, the world-class academic research that illuminates new directions, and the pioneering information science that puts all the pieces together.

Product Engineering—alongside clinical and big data—is one of three software disciplines at Sema4. We focus on the software used by patients, healthcare providers, partners, and payers. Sema4 seeks a talented Software Quality Engineer to help us change the way healthcare is delivered.

RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Build test infrastructure across multiple channels including web, mobile, service layers, back-end, and integration
  • Deeply embedded into the SDLC, the ideal candidate will be able to work closely and teach the development team on how to use this infrastructure to write tests themselves, creating an exponential improvement in the quality of our products.

QUALIFICATIONS:

  • Bachelors in Computer Science or solid on the job experience
  • 3+ years in a Quality Engineering role
  • Highly coachable and can take the vision and direction from our engineering leaders and make it a reality
  • Communicate clearly and often, both verbally and in writing
  • 3 + years’ experience with strong programming experience – Ruby on Rails and Javascript a plus
  • Experience shipping high quality software products (The healthcare tech space requires a standard of quality far higher than of traditional software products. The information we provide to our users affect their lives and thus our passion and processes for high quality software should reflect that)
  • Create tools and test infrastructure to ensure ownership of quality.
  • Facilitate testing in the development phase – Quality Engineers will use tools such as cypress.io to write UI tests AND teach the development team to use and write tests themselves, before their code is promoted.
