Software Engineer - 2019 College Grad

FI Works
-
Little Rock, AR 72223
$60,000 - $65,000 a year

For over 10 years we have been a pioneer in analyzing and acting on data as a FinTech company. We are a small, but fast-paced group constantly seeking innovative ways to push our software forward. You will spend 90% of your time in hands-on development with cutting-edge frameworks, tools, and services. This is an ideal opportunity if you are passionate about developing in Big Data, Data Analytics, AI, Data Management, and delivering actionable solutions with a great user experience. Our stack includes C#, ASP.NET, REST Services, HTML 5, JavaScript, CSS 3, Sass, SQL, and R.

Are you someone who can quickly learn and solve big problems? You will be given the opportunity to make a real impact in a highly collaborative culture. We are looking for individuals that are as passionate about building high-quality software as we are.

Responsibilities

  • Take end-to-end ownership of design and implementation of product features that will ship to customers
  • Write high-quality, clean, and maintainable code
  • Be a great teammate and enjoy our fun-loving positive culture
  • Build new product features across the back-end and front-end
  • Implement user experience improvements using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • Work with our development team to map technical requirements into architecture and technical design tasks
  • Participate in design and code reviews across the team

Requirements

  • May 2019 BS/MS Graduate (minimum 3.0 GPA) with strong Computer Science fundamentals
  • Excellent coding & debugging skills in an object-oriented language are a must (C# or Java preferred)
  • Strong data skills are a must with a thorough understanding of relational database design and writing SQL queries
  • Experience with web application development a plus with knowledge of MVC frameworks
  • Great communication skills and a strong-desire to learn new things
  • Strong analytical abilities, organizational skills, and attention to detail
  • High energy with an ability to work in a fast-paced environment
  • High initiative to take on projects that cover new ground and require self-learning

Bonus Qualifications

We do not expect anyone to cover all of these areas, but any experience or coursework specializations in any of these topics would get our attention:

  • Experience or knowledge in building AI and Machine Learning solutions (R or Python a plus)
  • Experience or knowledge in data warehousing would be plus
  • Experience or knowledge in multi-threading, message queues, and distributed computing would be a plus
  • Design skills from paper prototyping to implementing clean, consistent user experiences would be a plus
  • Data visualization experience or knowledge using Tableau, D3, or other tools

Benefits

  • Highly-competitive pay
  • Medical, dental, life and disability insurance
  • 401k
  • Opportunities for continued education and training
  • Flexible work schedule

At FI Works you will enjoy a highly-competitive compensation and benefits package. We are proud to support work/life balance with flexible work arrangements that meet individual needs. A casual dress code and above-average paid time off are just a few of the extra perks that make FI Works a great place to work.
